Let's Explore Pluto!

Pluto's Number

The Ninth Planet

As the ninth planet Pluto is the last planet in our solar system.


For some time we we looking for a planet beyond Pluto, but none was found.

Size

The Smallest Planet

Pluto is smaller than Earth's moon.


Due to its size Pluto is now called a dwarf planet.

Orbit

Pluto and Neptune

Sometimes Pluto moves closer to the sun than Neptune along its orbit.


Pluto and Neptune's orbits don't cross however and won't crash into each other.

Moons

It has Five Moons

Pluto has five moons: Charon, Hydra, Nix, Kerberos, and Styx.


Pluto's largest moon, Charon, is about half the size of Pluto.


The gravity between Pluto and Charon cause them to orbit around each other.
