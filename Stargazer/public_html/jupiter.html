Let's Explore Jupiter!

Jupiter's Number

The Fifth Planet

As the fifth planet Jupiter is the first of the outer planets.


Jupiter is further from the sun than Mars but closer than Saturn.

Size

The Largest Planet

Jupiter is the largest planet in the solar system with Saturn being the closest.


Despite this Jupiter is less than a tenth the size of the sun.

Surface

The Bands

Being a gas giant Jupiter's surface is covered in different colored bands of clouds.


The different color clouds are made up of different gasses.


The light bands are called zones while the darker bands are called belts.


The bands that are next to each other blow in opposite directions.

The Big Red Spot

It's a storm

Visible on Jupiter's surface the Big Red Spot is a huge storm in the clouds.


The Big Red Spot is large enough to hold two Earths.


This storm has persisted since before it was discovered.

Moons

Jupiter's Four Moons

Jupiter has four large moons.


They are Io, Europa, Ganymede, and Callisto.
