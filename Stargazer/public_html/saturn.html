Let's Explore Saturn!

Saturn's Number

The Sixth Planet

Saturn is the sixth planet in our solar system and second gas planet from the sun.


Saturn is further from the sun than Jupiter and closer than Uranus.

Color

Yellow

Saturn is a pale yellow color.


Jupiter may have brighter colors but Saturn is impressive in other ways.

Rings

The Rings

Saturn's rings are made of chunks of rock and ice orbiting around the planet.


While other planets have rings Saturn's are the brightest.

Moons

There's a Lot

Saturn has more than 50 moons, some of which are in its rings.


The gravity pull of these moons help keep the rings in place.

Size

And Shape

Saturn is the second largest planet in our solar system.


Strangely though Saturn is wider than it is tall.
