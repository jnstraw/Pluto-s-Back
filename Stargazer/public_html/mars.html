Let's Explore Mars!

Some facts about mars:

  • Mars has a reddish color
  • Mars is smaller than Earth. Mars is slightly larger than half of Earth's size.
  • Mars is the fourth planet from the sun.
  • Mars' orbit is slightly ellpitical (like a squished circle).
  • Mars doesn't have active plate tectonics.
  • Mars' southern hemishpere has a thicker crust than the northern half.
  • Mars has huge dust storms over its entire surface.
  • Mars has two ice caps, the northern one confirmed to have solid water and carbon dioxide (dry ice).
  • Mars is about 141,635,350 miles away from the sun.
  • Diameter = 88,008,126 miles
  • Mass 6.4219e23 kg
  • Temperatures range from -207 F to 80 F on Mars
  • Mars once had water 4 billion years ago
  • Mars’ atmosphere is very thin 95.3% carbon dioxide and 2.7% nitrogen.
  • Mars’ average pressure = 7 millibars (less than 1% of earth’s)
  • Mars has weak magnetic fields in various regions.
  • At night, during certain months of the martian year, mars is visible on earth to the unaided eye.
