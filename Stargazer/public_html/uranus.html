Let's Explore Jupiter!

Uranus's Number

The Seventh Planet

Uranus is the seventh planet in our solar system and third gas planet from the sun.


Uranus is further from the sun than Saturn and closer than Neptune.

Color

Blue-Green

Uranus is a gas planet with a blue-green color.


Uranus' color isn't as vivid as Neptune's deep blue color.

Axis

It's not up and down

Unlike Earth, Uranus's axis is nearly parallel to its orbit around the sun.


Because of this we don't know where Uranus's north pole is.

Core

What Core?

Most gas planets have small cores or none at all.


The rocky and icy material inside Uranus's gas is just floating about.
