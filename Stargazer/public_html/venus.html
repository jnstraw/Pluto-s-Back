Let's Explore Venus!

  • Venus is the second planet from the Sun
  • Venus is the one of the two planets without a moon
  • Venus is known as "Earth's Twin" because it is almost the same size
  • A year on Venus is 225 days on Earth
  • Venus is the hottest planet (even hotter than Mercury)
  • Venus is the brightest object in Earth's night sky
  • Venus has been nicknamed the "Morning Star" and "Evening Star"
  • A long time ago, people on Earth thought Venus was a tropical paradise, they were very wrong
  • Venus is covered in very thick clouds
  • Venus's clouds are the reason why Venus is so hot, they insulate the planet
  • If someone went to Venus, they clouds would kill them since they are poison
  • Like Mercury, a day on Venus lasts longer than a year on Venus
  • Venus was named after the Roman godess of love and beauty
  • Venus has a very weak magnetic field
  • Venus is the closest planet to Earth, being only 41,000,000 km (25,500,000 miles) away
  • Venus's orbit is the closeset of all planets to being a perfect circle
  • Because of the clouds, Venus is almost always having an intense storm
